Köppen Climate Maps

Kazakhstan’s and the World’s Köppen Climate Maps (CMIP6, CRU, ERA5 – GEE-based)

About the Project

This project is part of the doctoral research of Kalamkas Yessimkhanova, conducted under the supervision of Dr. Mátyás Gede at Eötvös Loránd University in Budapest.

It leverages Google Earth Engine and a variety of climate datasets to generate Köppen Climate Classification maps. These maps classify the climate of Kazakhstan and the world based on long-term monthly averages of temperature and precipitation.
