🧪 About the Project

This project is part of my doctoral research at Eötvös Loránd University. It uses Google Earth Engine and CMIP6 climate data to map the future Köppen climate classifications of Kazakhstan. The app provides interactive access to both historical baselines and projected changes under SSP2-4.5 and SSP5-8.5 scenarios.
